      What is a Cue?
    • An exact reminder to do something; An exact time or moment
    • Good Examples: At 8am every morning | When I pass through a doorway | After I start the laundry machine
    • Bad Examples: In the morning | when I'm in the house | when I do the laundry
      What is a Response?
    • The smallest, most immediate action to do
    • Good examples: put on my gym clothes | think a positive thought | set a timer
    • Bad examples: Go to the gym | Be happy | Remember to turn the laundry over
      So what is an Atomic Habit?
    • The smallest possible routine actions. Atomic habits are powerful tools for personal growth.
